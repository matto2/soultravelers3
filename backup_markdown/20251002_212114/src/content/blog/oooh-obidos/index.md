This is such a darling little town that makes you want to just "oooh" and "ahhh" every few seconds at the visual impact of the adorableness everywhere. I had read that this was the prettiest village in Portugal, but I was still surprised to find it so enchanting. We saw it late in the day ( not great for some of the pics)Â  when there were no tourists and just fell in love with it! Sadly, our pictures just couldn't capture the magic, so you will just have to go and see it yourselves!
